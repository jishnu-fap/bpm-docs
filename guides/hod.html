Head of Department (HOD)

How to assign a Head of Department to each zone or department, end an existing assignment, and view the history of all past HODs per zone.

1
Admin
View the HOD page — zones and current assignments
Navigate to HOD in the sidebar. All zones and departments are listed in a collapsible tree. Each row shows the currently active HOD — their avatar, name, and email. Rows with no assignment show None assigned. Expand a zone to see its sub-departments.

2
Admin
Assign an HOD to a zone
Click Assign on any row that has no current HOD. The Assign HOD modal opens — select a user from the system and specify the start date of their appointment. The assignment takes effect immediately and the row updates to show the new HOD's details.

Admin
End an HOD assignment
Click End on a zone that has an active HOD. The End HOD modal asks for an end date and an optional reason (e.g. resignation, rotation, restructure). Once confirmed, the zone shows as None assigned and an Assign button reappears. The ended record moves to history.

4
Admin
View HOD history for a zone
Click History on any zone row (regardless of whether an HOD is currently assigned) to see the full list of past appointments. The history shows who held the position, their start and end dates, the reason for ending, and whether the appointment is still active.

5
Admin
Reassign after ending — completing a rotation
After ending an assignment, click Assign on the same zone to appoint the next HOD. This is the standard workflow for periodic rotations — end the current HOD's appointment with the rotation date as the end date, then immediately assign the new one with the same date as their start.
Only users with the HOD assign permission can perform these actions. The permission is role-based and configured in the Roles page.
