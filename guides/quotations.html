Approval workflow
3
Sales / Admin
Submit for approval
From the quotation detail page, click Submit for Approval. The status changes from Draft to Pending Approval and the quotation is locked from further edits until a decision is made.
QT-2025-0041 · v1 Draft
Submit for Approval →
Once submitted, edits are locked until reviewed.
4
Approver (Admin / HOD)
Approve or reject the quotation
The approver sees all Pending Approval quotations in the list. From the detail page they can Approve or Reject with an optional reason. The submitter can see the decision and reason in the quotation detail.

5
Sales / Admin
Revise a rejected quotation (create new version)
If the quotation is rejected, the submitter can click Revise on the detail page. A new version is created (v2, v3, …) — the original remains as a historical record. The revised draft follows the same submit → approve cycle.
All versions of a quotation share the same reference number so you can track the full negotiation history.
Conversion to contract
6
Admin
Convert an approved quotation to a contract
Once approved, click Convert to Contract on the quotation detail page. The system creates a new contract pre-populated with the quotation's client, project, and line-item details. The quotation status changes to Converted and becomes read-only.
